How do I cancel my order?
You can cancel your order by logging into your Open Medicine account and clicking on "Order History". Click on the details of the order you wish to cancel and hit the "Cancel" button. If your order is "In Transit" you must wait until the order reaches you and if you still wish to cancel, simply tell the courier delivery person that you "refuse delivery", and the order will then cancel.
